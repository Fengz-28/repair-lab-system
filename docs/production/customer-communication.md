# Customer Communication Policy

Actualizado: 2026-06-19, America/Costa_Rica.

## Current channel decision

FengzLab communication is email-first for transactional customer updates.

WhatsApp is deferred until there is a dedicated WhatsApp Business number. The owner's personal WhatsApp should not be treated as the official public channel by default.

## Source of truth

The customer portal remains the tracking source. Email notifications should point customers back to the portal instead of trying to duplicate all ticket details in the email body.

## Product catalog decision

Products remain a consultative catalog for now. Do not add ecommerce checkout, cart, payment gateway, or automated purchase flow in this phase.

## Message tone

Messages should be:

- short;
- professional;
- customer-safe;
- clear about the next step;
- free of internal technical notes.

## Deferred channels

Deferred until explicitly approved:

- WhatsApp Business Cloud API;
- n8n automations;
- SMS;
- marketing campaigns;
- AI-generated auto-replies without human review.
